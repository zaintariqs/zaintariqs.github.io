What is HAWALE

Hawalay is Pakistan’s first decentralized physical infrastructure network (DePIN) for crypto point-of-sale (PoS) payments. We empower merchants to accept digital assets like Bitcoin, stablecoins, and more, while earning rewards in our native token for operating network-powered hardware. Our mission is to bring financial access, transparency, and innovation to small and large businesses across the country.

DePIN Crypto POS – Customer Segments for the Pakistani Market

  • Small Retailers & Kiryana Stores

    Neighborhood shops with limited banking support. Open to mobile payments and eager to save on fees. HAWALAY helps them accept stablecoins and earn tokens as DePIN nodes.

  • Dhaba Owners & Food Vendors

Why Choose HAWALAY?

Instant Settlements

Accept multiple cryptos against goods and services - settle accounts in PKR instantly without having to worry about fluctuating exchange rates. Experience instant crypto settlement with lightning-fast, secure transactions.

Lower Transaction Fees

Traditional payment processors (like Visa or Mastercard) typically charge 2% to 5% per transaction, eating into your profits. In Pakistan, small businesses often avoid digital payments due to high fees. Hawalay’s crypto PoS flips the script—cheaper, faster, smarter.

DePIN Advantage

Merchants using DePIN Crypto PoS systems can unlock multiple earning factors beyond just selling goods. Unlike traditional PoS terminals, a DePIN-enabled PoS can participate in the underlying token economy and infrastructure layer, offering new revenue streams and incentives.
