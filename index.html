What is HAWALE

Hawalay is Pakistan’s first decentralized physical infrastructure network (DePIN) for crypto point-of-sale (PoS) payments. We empower merchants to accept digital assets like Bitcoin, stablecoins, and more, while earning rewards for operating network-powered hardware. Our mission is to bring financial access, transparency, and innovation to small and large businesses across the country.

How it works

1. Device Setup

The merchant installs a DePIN POS device and registers it on the blockchain network, receiving a unique wallet address.

2. Network Sync

The POS syncs with the blockchain to retrieve smart contracts, current exchange rates, and validate the device.

3. Customer Checkout

The customer selects goods or services. The POS calculates the price in cryptocurrency using live data.

4. Payment Request

The POS generates a QR code or NFC request with the amount due in crypto tokens.

5. Customer Pays

The customer scans the QR and confirms the transaction from their crypto wallet.

6. Transaction Verification

The transaction is broadcast to the blockchain and confirmed in a few blocks.

7. Funds Received

The merchant's wallet receives the crypto payment, and a receipt is generated.

8. Rewards & Logging

The DePIN network logs the transaction and may reward the POS operator with tokens based on activity or uptime.

9. Tax and Audit
